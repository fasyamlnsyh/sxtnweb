About

Visual systems rooted in clear concepts and thoughtful execution.

Sxtn Studio™ is an experimental type and graphic design studio based in Jakarta, Indonesia. We work across visual identity, typography, branding, packaging, and creative direction, developing visual systems that are rooted in clear concepts and thoughtful execution. Every project starts with understanding the purpose behind the work, allowing each outcome to have its own character while remaining relevant to the people it is made for.

Our practice combines research, experimentation, and craftsmanship throughout the design process. We are interested in exploring typography, composition, and contemporary visual culture to create work that feels distinctive without losing its function. By working closely with brands, businesses, and creative individuals, we aim to build visual identities and design experiences that communicate with clarity and leave a lasting impression.
